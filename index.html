Central Argument

Resident bypass is not a supervisory style. This case documents it as a patient safety failure mode with a reproducible mechanism, a traceable causal chain from bypass decision to patient-level harm, and an institutional detection gap that existing supervisory and reporting frameworks are structurally unable to address.
